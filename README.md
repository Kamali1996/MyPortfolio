## Featured Artifacts

### 1. Rachel: AI-Powered Health Benefits Assistant
An applied AI project demonstrating the safe and structured use of Generative AI in the highly regulated healthcare space. 
* **Objective:** Serve as a first-line support tool for health insurance members to parse Explanation of Benefits (EOB) documents and summarize policy tiers.
* **QA Focus:** Rigorously tested against negative scenarios (e.g., PHI/PII injection, unauthorized medical advice) to ensure the AI remains strictly within its designated guardrails without hallucinating external data.

### 2. Machine Learning vs. Deep Learning in Healthcare
A strategic architectural analysis comparing traditional Machine Learning and Deep Learning models to solve specific healthcare insurance business problems.
* **Objective:** Differentiate between ML (Customer Churn Prediction) and DL (NLP Transcript Analysis) use cases by mapping the correct technology to specific data architectures and business needs.
* **QA & Business Focus:** Demonstrates the ability to evaluate underlying data structures (structured vs. unstructured), computational costs, and the necessity for model explainability to recommend the most efficient and secure AI architecture.
